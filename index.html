什麼是 Serendipity？

(n.) The art of discovering valuable things from unexpected occurrences.

The word was inspired by a Persian fairy tale called “The Three Princes of Serendip.”

As the Highnesses travelled, they were always making discoveries of things they were not in quest of by accidents and sagacity.
